What is a Digital Induction?

Congratulations on your new position at NTU!  You may be new to NTU or you may be an existing employee new to another role.  As part of your induction, it is important that you are introduced to the core digital tools and systems that you will encounter during your career at NTU.  The Digital Induction will enable you to orientate yourself around these and help you to grow your awareness and confidence in their use.  There are a range of structured activities to get you up to speed with the tools and systems you will most likely use in your new role.

Getting started

To get you started on your digital journey, select First Day from the options below.  We recommend that the First Day and First Week activities are completed within your first week in your new role.  After you have completed your First Day and First Week activities you can then progress through to the First Month and Three Months.   As you progress you will be able to collect a range of digital badges that recognise you have completed all activities relating to that stage.  You can dip in and out, but do make sure that you complete each activity as this will go towards the badge for the relevant stage.  Once you have gained a badge, you will then be able to view them by selecting More > scroll down to Awards > My Awards.

Instructions

Look out for the icons below, these indicate and action you need to undertake as part of your induction.

Information - this indicates need to know information on how to continue through this induction or that may relate to your role.

Activity - requires you to participate in a structured task to familiarise you with the digital tools and systems available at NTU.

Review - a prompt for you to discuss your progress with your line manager as part of the formal induction/probation process.
